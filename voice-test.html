This connects puck.js via Web Bluetooth and then uses Annyang to interpret speech from the mic and issue commands.

    Commands Supported:
  • 'Turn on'
  • 'Turn off'
  • 'Get Light Sensor'
  • 'Get Temperature'
  • 'Get Battery'
  • 'Find Label'
  • 'Remove Label'
  • 'Add Label'
  • 'Show Email'
